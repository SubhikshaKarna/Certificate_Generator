Certificate Generator Help

Getting Started

If you're new to the Certificate Generator, follow these steps to get started:

  1. Select a template: Choose from the available certificate templates.
  2. Enter Names: Provide the names for the certificates.
  3. Generate Certificates: Click the button to generate the certificates.
  4. Download Certificates: Download the generated certificates to your device.

FAQs

How to upload a custom template?

To upload a custom template, click on the "Upload Image" button and select your template image.
